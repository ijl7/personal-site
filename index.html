Site Info

This site was primarily built as a passion project, in order to hone my skills and also show expansion into new areas, since as previously mentioned, many school projects are more focused on back-end technology. Building this site involved a fair amount of research, not only into new coding languages, but how to purchase a domain and manage the site as well.

However, this website was not simply built as resume padding. It further allowed me to see how managing my time on a project would work, and also allowed for building of my creativity and problem solving, as I attempted to incorporate features that are both aesthetically pleasing and useful.
